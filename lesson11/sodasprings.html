Test Your Fire Alarms

Late November brought in a crazy winter storm for the Wendy houshold. The temperatures dropped to -20 degrees with a wind chill during the day. The residence of the Wendy family are all laying in bed fast asleep. All of a sudden the parents are bolted awake to a fire alarm and a smoke filled house. Quickly the area was scouted out and the source of this smoke is the furnace. The furnace is smoking and the metal has changed color to black. Upon turning off the furnace the fire alarms are checked as only one of the 4 fire alarms alerted the residence. Why did not all the fire alarms perform their tasks and alert the residence? This can only be found out by performing tests of the alarms.
