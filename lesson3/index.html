Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide weather planning for the local farming/ranching industry. Planning the irrigation of the crops depends greatly on the weather. This site will provide that needed location where the locals can go to gather the information they need to plan their days. As for ranchers the need of supplementing feed for the cattle depends on the temperature and the ease the cattle can get down to and eat the stubble. This site will provide that needed location to go and prepare for the coming winter days.
